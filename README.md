## 🚀 Selected Projects

### ML and AI

- [Reasoning Model Failure Analysis Companion](https://github.com/abhijeetgupta02/reasoning-model-failure-analysis-companion) — sanitized public companion for forced-reconsideration LLM failure analysis with synthetic goal-betrayal and partial-undo artifacts, no private prompts, and no raw model outputs.
- [Adversarial RL Feature Selection](https://github.com/abhijeetgupta02/adversarial-rl-feature-selection) — public reconstruction with published-result separation, entropy / mutual-information diagnostics, and explicit `NOT_RUN` PPO/ARS experiments.
- [Mouse Brain Cell Segmentation](https://github.com/abhijeetgupta02/mouse-brain-cell-segmentation) — COCO-style public companion with synthetic six-class fluorescence evaluation and private-data boundaries.
- [Virtual Yoga Instructor](https://github.com/abhijeetgupta02/virtual-yoga-instructor) — MediaPipe-compatible feedback engine with eight joint angles, TCP/JSON packets, and geometry latency evidence.
- [Career Prediction Benchmark](https://github.com/abhijeetgupta02/career-prediction-benchmark) — privacy-safe multi-output benchmark reconstruction with 26-to-11 entropy feature selection and McNemar / Cochran-Q significance testing.
- [AI for Construction Safety](https://github.com/abhijeetgupta02/ai-for-construction-safety) — GROVE evidence package with F1 `0.7591`, paired tests, Wilson / Clopper-Pearson sparse-category intervals, threshold sensitivity, and claim traceability.

### Quantitative Research

- [Options Pricing and Greeks](https://github.com/abhijeetgupta02/options-pricing-and-greeks) — Black-Scholes, first- through third-order Greeks (vanna, vomma, charm, speed, zomma, color), Monte Carlo variance reduction, no-arbitrage tests, and implied-volatility surface recovery.
- [Statistical Pairs Trading](https://github.com/abhijeetgupta02/statistical-pairs-trading) — leakage-controlled AAPL-MSFT, KO-PEP, XOM-CVX backtests (only KO-PEP passes the 5% cointegration gate), with Hurst, variance-ratio, and Kalman hedge-ratio mean-reversion diagnostics.
- [GARCH Volatility Modeling](https://github.com/abhijeetgupta02/garch-volatility-modeling) — daily one-step forecasts where GARCH wins RMSE on 4/6 symbols and QLIKE on 5/6, with Mincer-Zarnowitz calibration and HAC Diebold-Mariano tests.
- [Financial Time-Series Forecasting](https://github.com/abhijeetgupta02/financial-time-series-forecasting) — ARIMA, ML baselines, and repeated LSTM seeds (ARIMA wins MAE/RMSE in the verified run), with Diebold-Mariano and Theil U2 significance tests.
